Paneer Thali

In the heart of bustling Indian neighborhoods, the aroma of spices intertwines with the rhythm of daily life, offering a tantalizing glimpse into the culinary tapestry of the subcontinent. Amidst the vibrant chaos, the paneer thali emerges as a beacon of tradition and flavor. Adorned with a colorful array of dishes, from creamy paneer tikka masala to aromatic saag paneer, each bite tells a story of centuries-old culinary heritage. Crisp papadums crackle in anticipation, while fluffy naan bread beckons with its comforting warmth. Amidst the medley of flavors, the tangy sweetness of mango chutney and the fiery kick of pickled onions dance on the palate, offering a symphony of sensations. In this gastronomic journey, paneer takes center stage, its soft texture and mild taste complementing the bold spices and rich gravies of the thali. As diners savor each morsel, they become part of a timeless tradition, where every meal is a celebration of culture, community, and the culinary artistry of India.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Mix together crumbled paneer, flour, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.

step 2

In a separate bowl, whisk milk, eggs, melted butter, sugar, and vanilla extract.

step 3

Gradually add the wet mixture to the dry mixture, stirring until just combined. Cook pancakes on a greased pan until golden brown on both sides

ingredients

250g paneer, crumbled

1 cup all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

Hand Blender
